## FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**The Official Countdown 250 Ball to Serve as Formal Evening Finale of Great American State Fair Week at Historic Washington Hilton**

*Independence Eve black-tie gala brings 3,000 guests to the capital for a midnight countdown to America's 250th year — three blocks from the National Mall festival*

**WASHINGTON, D.C.** — The Official Countdown 250 Ball, the premier gala launching America's 250th anniversary weekend, will take place on the evening of Friday, July 3, 2026 at the iconic Washington Hilton. The date places the Ball squarely in the middle of the recently announced Great American State Fair, the 16-day Freedom 250 festival running June 25 through July 10 on the National Mall.

The Ball offers Fair visitors, DC-area residents, and out-of-town guests a formal evening complement to the Fair's daytime programming. Three blocks from the Mall at 1919 Connecticut Avenue NW, 3,000 guests will gather in black-tie attire for an extraordinary evening of patriotism, entertainment, and celebration — ending with a signature midnight countdown marking the exact instant America enters its 250th year.

"The Great American State Fair announcement reshapes the landscape for DC visitors planning their 250th anniversary trip," said Mike Harrigan, of the All-American Ball Committee, the event's organizer. "Families and travelers can experience the Fair by day on the Mall, then close out Independence Eve in black tie at the Washington Hilton. It's the pairing a generation has been waiting for."

"Fifty years ago, Washington hosted the Bicentennial," Harrigan added. "On July 3, 2026, we honor that legacy and launch the next fifty years. Every guest will walk out of the Washington Hilton with the greatest memories of their lives."
