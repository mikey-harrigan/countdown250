## As New York Restricts Access to Iconic Ball Drop, Washington, D.C. Invites All Americans to Celebrate at the Official Countdown 250 Ball

*Three thousand guests to mark America's 250th anniversary at the legendary Washington Hilton on July 3, 2026*

---

**WASHINGTON, D.C.** — As New York City transforms its iconic Times Square New Year's Eve celebration into a ticketed, limited-access event, the All-American Ball Committee announced that the Official Countdown 250 Ball will welcome up to 3,000 guests for a historic evening marking the 250th anniversary of the United States.

The black-tie gala, set for the evening of July 3, 2026, at the legendary Washington Hilton in the nation's capital, offers Americans a guaranteed, all-inclusive celebration — with no lottery, no restrictions, and no standing in the cold.

"America's 250th birthday belongs to everyone," said Mike Harrigan, Chairman of the All-American Ball Committee. "While other iconic celebrations are becoming harder to access, we're throwing the doors open. If you want to be in the room when history is made, there's a place for you at the Countdown 250 Ball."

### A Once-in-a-Generation Celebration

The Countdown 250 Ball spans the Washington Hilton's grand event spaces and features:

- **Six themed ballrooms**, each with its own ambiance and entertainment
- **Four live entertainment stages** with bands and DJs performing throughout the evening
- **Premium open bars** and culinary stations featuring world-class cuisine
- **The All-American Hero Awards**, honoring outstanding Americans
- **A midnight countdown** launching America's 250th birthday, broadcast live

The Washington Hilton has hosted presidents, world leaders, and the White House Correspondents' Dinner for over 60 years, making it the definitive setting for a celebration of this magnitude.

### Tickets Available Now

Unlike New York's newly restricted celebration, tickets to the Countdown 250 Ball are available now and guarantee entry:

- **Liberty Pass** ($195) — Full ball experience, 9 PM to 1 AM
- **Patriot VIP Pass** ($450) — VIP reception and ball, 7 PM to 1 AM
- **Statesman's Concierge Pass** ($1,700) — Premium all-access experience with reserved Presidential Stage seating, private entrance, and dedicated concierge service

Tickets are available at [countdown250.allamericanball.com](https://countdown250.allamericanball.com).

### About the All-American Ball Committee

For more than 30 years, the All-American Ball Committee has produced premiere celebrations in Washington, D.C., including multiple inaugural balls and milestone galas. Past events have welcomed dignitaries, military leaders, celebrities, and thousands of proud Americans. The Countdown 250 Ball benefits the Operation Renewed Hope Foundation, supporting housing for U.S. veterans.
