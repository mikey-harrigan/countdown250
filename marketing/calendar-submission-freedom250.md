**Event 1: The Official Countdown 250 Ball**
Friday, July 3, 2026 — Washington Hilton — 7:00 PM to 1:00 AM

An Independence Eve gala for 3,000 guests built around a midnight countdown marking the exact instant America enters its 250th year. This event runs during the Great American State Fair (June 25 – July 10) on the National Mall and offers the fair's visitors a formal evening option just three blocks away. The program includes the All-American Hero Lifetime Achievement Awards, a patriotic tribute, six party zones, and four entertainment stages. Features non-partisan, multigenerational programming aligned with the Freedom 250 "Triumph of the American Spirit" theme.

Event website: https://countdown250.allamericanball.com
Ticketing: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/1982528927390

**Event 2: Pre-Prix 250**
Saturday, August 22, 2026 — Washington Hilton — 6:00 PM to Midnight

A racing-themed black-tie gala held the evening of the Freedom 250 Grand Prix of Washington, D.C. Designed as the formal social anchor of the INDYCAR race weekend. Hospitality tiers built for motorsports fans, corporate partners, and out-of-town attendees. Provides a non-compete, same-evening social experience for guests already in DC for the race.

Event website: https://preprix250.com
