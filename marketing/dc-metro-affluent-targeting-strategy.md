## CHANNEL-BY-CHANNEL STRATEGY

### 1. META (FACEBOOK + INSTAGRAM) — GEO-TARGETED ADS

**Setup:**
- Create Saved Audience in Meta Ads Manager (can do NOW, doesn't need pixel data)
- Location: Target each zip code listed above
- Age: 30-65
- Income: Top 10% household income (Meta allows this)
- Interests: Black tie events, galas, philanthropy, wine/spirits, luxury travel, opera, Kennedy Center, White House Correspondents' Dinner
- Job titles: CEO, CFO, VP, Director, Partner, Managing Director, Colonel+, Admiral, General

**Ad Sets (run simultaneously for frequency):**
1. **Awareness ad** — Cinematic ballroom image + "The Official Countdown 250 Ball — July 3, Washington Hilton" — $10/day
2. **Social proof ad** — "30+ years of premier events. 3,000 guests. 6 ballrooms." — $10/day  
3. **Urgency ad** — "Early pricing ends soon. Don't miss America's 250th." — $10/day
4. **Statesman's ad** — "The Statesman's Concierge Experience — $1,700. Reserved Presidential Stage seating." — $10/day targeting only HHI $200K+

**Total: $40/day across 4 ad sets = same person sees you 3-4x per week**

### 2. NEXTDOOR — NEIGHBORHOOD-LEVEL TARGETING

**Why:** Nextdoor lets you target by specific neighborhoods. Wealthy DC suburbs are VERY active on Nextdoor. It's trusted because it's neighbors talking to neighbors.

**How:**
- Create a Nextdoor Business page for the All-American Ball Committee
- Post in the "Events" section for each target neighborhood
- Run Nextdoor Ads (they offer hyper-local targeting by neighborhood)
- Targeting: Great Falls, McLean, Potomac, Bethesda, Chevy Chase, Old Town Alexandria, Georgetown
- Ad format: Local Deals or Sponsored Posts
- Budget: ~$500-1,000 total, spread across neighborhoods
- Copy angle: "Your neighbors in [Great Falls/McLean/Potomac] are going. Are you?"

## THE FREQUENCY PLAY — HOW THIS ALL WORKS TOGETHER

The goal: A person in Great Falls sees "Countdown 250 Ball" in 7+ different contexts over 4-6 weeks:

1. **Monday AM** — Hears radio spot on WTOP during commute
2. **Monday lunch** — Sees Facebook ad while scrolling
3. **Tuesday** — Neighbor posts about it in Great Falls Facebook group
4. **Wednesday** — Sees ad in McLean Connection newspaper
5. **Thursday** — Instagram ad while browsing
6. **Friday** — Gets premium invitation card in the mail
7. **Saturday** — Sees it mentioned in Nextdoor
8. **Following week** — Spotify ad during their run
9. **At the club** — Sees a flyer at Congressional Country Club

By touchpoint 5-6, they're Googling "Countdown 250 Ball" — and your Google Ad is there to catch them. By touchpoint 7-8, they're buying tickets.

**This is how you fill 3,000 seats.**
