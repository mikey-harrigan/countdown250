## Orchestration

Don't send all three at once. Sequence them:

1. **Week 1–2:** Send all 50 Template 1 (personal invitations). These are warmest and highest-ROI per send.
2. **Week 2–3:** Send Template 2 to 10 corporate firms. Needs research on right contact per firm.
3. **Week 3–4:** Recruit ambassadors via Template 3. Select 20 names from your network, send, hold calls with those who bite.

Track every send in a simple spreadsheet: name, email, date sent, date followed up, response, outcome. Conversion rate on Template 1 (personal) should be 10–20%. On Template 2 (corporate) 2–5%. On Template 3 (ambassador) 30% say "let's talk," 10% become active ambassadors.

## Math check

- 50 Template 1 × 15% conversion × $1,700 = **$12,750 if one buys average 1 pass; higher if most buy 2+**
- 10 Template 2 × 3% conversion × $15,000 average package = **$4,500 expected, but one Presidential Host at $18,500 pays for the whole effort**
- 20 Template 3 × 40% engagement × 3 referrals average × $450 avg ticket = **$10,800 from downstream referrals**

Total expected direct outreach value: **~$28,000+** for one week of sending. Not bad for zero paid media.
