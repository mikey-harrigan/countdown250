========================================
STEP 2: PR NEWSWIRE (PAID - ~$400-800)
========================================

This sends your press release to thousands of newsrooms, AP, Google News,
Yahoo News, Bing News, and hundreds of local/national outlets automatically.

HOW TO DO IT:
1. Go to: https://www.prnewswire.com
2. Click "Send a Press Release" or "Get Started"
3. Create an account
4. Choose "US National" distribution (~$800) or "Local - DC/Mid-Atlantic" (~$400)
5. Paste in the full press release text
6. Add the event logo as an image
7. Choose your distribution date (sooner = better)
8. Pay and submit

TIPS:
- "Local - DC/Mid-Atlantic" is the best value for your event
- Add the logo/event image — releases with images get 3x more engagement
- Schedule for a Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday morning (best pickup days)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays (news gets buried)
