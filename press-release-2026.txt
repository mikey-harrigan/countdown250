FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

ALL-AMERICAN COUNTDOWN 250 BALL TO HOST 3,000 GUESTS AT WASHINGTON HILTON
FOR HISTORIC CELEBRATION LAUNCHING AMERICA'S 250TH ANNIVERSARY WEEKEND

Once-in-a-Generation Black-Tie Gala Features Live Entertainment Across 4 Stages,
6 Party Zones, Midnight Countdown, and the All-American Hero Lifetime Achievement Awards

WASHINGTON, D.C. — The All-American Countdown 250 Ball, the premier gala event launching America's 250th anniversary weekend, will take place on the evening of July 3, 2026 at the iconic Washington Hilton in Washington, D.C. An expected 3,000 guests — including dignitaries, military leaders, media personalities, and distinguished citizens from across the nation — will gather for an extraordinary evening of patriotism, entertainment, and celebration as the nation counts down to its historic semiquincentennial.

"This is the event of a generation," said Mike Harrigan, Founder of GoCity Group, the event's organizer. "On the eve of America's 250th birthday, we are bringing together thousands of Americans from every walk of life for a night they will never forget. This will be a carnival of the senses — an evening of spectacle, patriotism, and pure celebration unlike anything Washington has ever seen."

"Our goal is simple," Harrigan added. "We want every single guest to walk out of the Washington Hilton with the greatest memories of their lives. Every detail — from the entertainment to the midnight countdown to the way we treat our guests — is being designed to deliver an experience that is truly once in a lifetime."

A CARNIVAL OF THE SENSES

The Countdown 250 Ball promises an unparalleled sensory experience spanning the Washington Hilton's grand event spaces. Guests will enjoy:

- LIVE ENTERTAINMENT ACROSS 4 STAGES: Multiple live bands, DJs, and performers delivering nonstop energy from doors open until the final note.

- 6 IMMERSIVE PARTY ZONES: Each themed zone offers a unique atmosphere — from high-energy dance floors to elegant lounges — ensuring every guest finds their perfect experience.

- PREMIUM OPEN BAR ALL NIGHT: A full premium open bar available to all ticket holders throughout the evening.

- A MIDNIGHT COUNTDOWN LIKE NO OTHER: Forget the standard ball drop or balloon release. The Countdown 250 midnight experience is being designed as a breathtaking, multi-sensory production — a cinematic moment of patriotic grandeur that will mark the exact instant America enters its 250th year. This is not New Year's Eve. This is the beginning of America's next chapter, and every guest will remember exactly where they stood when the clock struck midnight.

- ROAMING ENTERTAINERS AND IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES: Performers, interactive installations, and surprises throughout the venue creating unforgettable moments at every turn.

- PATRIOTIC TRIBUTE TO AMERICA: A stirring performance honoring 250 years of American history, courage, and achievement.

ALL-AMERICAN HERO LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARDS

A highlight of the evening will be the presentation of the All-American Hero Lifetime Achievement Awards, recognizing extraordinary Americans whose lifetime of service, sacrifice, and leadership embodies the best of the nation. Past honorees have included decorated military veterans, pioneering first responders, groundbreaking educators, and tireless community leaders.

The public is invited to nominate deserving individuals for the 2026 awards. Nominees can come from any background — military, first responders, healthcare, education, community service, or public leadership. What matters is a lifetime of extraordinary service to others.
